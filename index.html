Who We Are
A family business built on trust.

Tanner Construction is a family-owned basement finishing company with over 30 years of experience. We believe in doing things the right way: transparent pricing, honest communication, and quality craftsmanship on every project. When you work with us, you're working directly with the owners who stake their family name on every basement we finish. After decades of success in California, we've moved to Utah and are ready to bring that same expertise to homeowners here. No surprises. No runaround. Just a basement finished right.

Hunting Tanner

Founder / Licensed General Contractor

Hunting is a third-generation contractor who grew up on job sites working alongside his dad. He earned his degree in Finance from BYU, combining hands-on building experience with strong business fundamentals. As the licensed GC in Utah, Hunting handles everything that keeps your project moving: estimates, contracts, scheduling, inspections, client communication, and financial management. He's on site daily, making sure every detail is handled and every question gets answered.
